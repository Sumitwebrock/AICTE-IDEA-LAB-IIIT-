About the Lab

AICTE-IDEA (Idea Development, Evaluation & Application) Labs are being established across the country to encourage students to apply STEM fundamentals towards enhanced hands-on experience.

At IIIT Naya Raipur, our IDEA Lab serves as a common facility embedded in the institution to make engineering graduates more imaginative and creative. It provides a 24x7 collaborative space for converting ideas into prototypes.
